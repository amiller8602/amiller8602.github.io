Best Blueberry Cheesecake Recipe

Here's what you'll need:

  1. Two cups of graham cracker crumbs
  2. A third of a cup of melted butter
  3. One and a fourth cups of sugar
  4. Twenty-four ounces of softened cream cheese
  5. One cup of sour cream
  6. Two teaspoons of vanilla
  7. Three eggs
  8. Twenty-one ounces of blueberry pie filling (Where to buy blueberry pie filling)

Here's the Steps:

  • Heat your oven to 350 degrees
  • Mix the graham cracker crumbs, the butter, and the sugar. Then press onto the bottom of a 9 inch pan
  • Mix the cream cheese and 1/4 cup of the sugar with a mixer. Then add the sour cream and vanilla and continue to mix
  • Add the eggs to the mixture one at a time until blended. Pour the mixture over the crust
  • Bake for one hour, then refrigerate for four hours
  • Top with the blueberry pie filling, and serve

And that's all! Enjoy your blueberry cheesecake!
